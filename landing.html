GameVault is offline-first. By default, your data stays in your browser's secure HTML5 IndexedDB storage database. Once you save Supabase credentials, mutations are piped to your remote database.

Standard tables crash or lag when rendering 10,000+ items. Our virtualization engine only renders the 15-20 rows visible in the viewport, reusing table cells during scroll. This guarantees a smooth 60fps experience.
